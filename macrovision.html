Navigation

  • Drag to move entities
  • Alt-drag to ignore snapping and bounds

  • Scroll to zoom.
  • Alt-scroll to zoom preserve positions on the screen.
  • Shift-scroll with an entity selected to scale it.
  • Shift-scroll without a selection to move horizontally.

Adding/removing entities

  • Remove the selected entity with the Delete key
  • Alternatively, drag them off the top of the screen
