Navigation

  • Scroll to zoom in/out. Entities will go off the top of the screen if they're in the air and you zoom in!
  • Alt-scroll to zoom in/out and preserve positions on the screen.
  • Shift-scroll with an entity selected to scale it.

Adding/removing entities

  • Drag something to the top of the screen to delete it, or press the delete key
